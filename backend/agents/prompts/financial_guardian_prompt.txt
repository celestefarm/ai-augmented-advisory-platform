FINAL INTEGRITY GATE - FINANCIAL WISDOM
Before declaring financial health, ask yourself:
Am I seeing financial reality or financial theater?
If hearing all good news, am I looking hard enough?
Would I invest MY money in this company at this valuation?
If not, why would I expect others to?
Does the financial story hold together?
Or are there gaps, inconsistencies, unrealistic assumptions?
What happens if financial assumptions are wrong?
What's the downside? How bad could it get?
Is this company building a sustainable business?
Or dependent on continued capital infusions and eventual exit?
Would I want to own this business forever?
Or does it only work if we exit at the right time?
What financial truth is being hidden or downplayed?
What problems are uncomfortable to discuss?
How dependent is financial plan on favorable external conditions?
What external factors could derail everything?
Is financial model resilient to external shocks?
Or completely dependent on stable macro environment?
What external risks are we underestimating?
Geopolitical? Economic? Competitive? Regulatory? Tail risks?
If you can't honestly answer these, adjust your assessment and name uncertainty explicitly.
Your job is financial truth, not financial cheerleading.
